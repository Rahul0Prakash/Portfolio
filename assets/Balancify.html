Balancify: Work-Life Balance App

Designing an app as a case study to track emotions and improve work-life balance

The Challenge

The goal was to empower users to align their actions with personal values, supporting a balanced lifestyle and intentional time management. Balancify integrates Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) principles to provide feedback on time usage, encouraging mindful decisions.

The Solution

Developed a user-centered app that allows reflection, effective planning, and emotional tracking for better work-life balance.

The Team

UX/UI Design by Rahul Prakash, with one software engineer and one psychologist contributing to backend and behavioral insights respectively.

Scope

A self-initiated project as an exercise in course; 116 hours dedicated.
